2025 Burdiss Board Game Tournament

Saturday January 25, 2025
10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
$10 Entry Fee

Event Details

Half of the registration cost will go towards lunch and snacks.

Registration is limited to the first 16 people. The first 16 people to register and pay their fees will be added to a 16 person bracket.

All games will use the base game only, no expansions. Each bracket match will be played at the 2 player count only.

Games will be randomly assigned to each player in the bracket. Once advancing, players will never play the same game twice. For the final round, each finalist will be able to veto one game from the remaining games in the pool, and a random game will be selected from the last available games.

Prizes

Each round of the tournament, every winner will recieve a $5 prize. The more games you win, the more money you will recieve.

The grand prize for winning the whole tournament is $10 ($25 total from each preceding round prizes).

The Games

Azul

 Learn to Play Azul
  • Players will play the preprinted side of the board.
  • When filling factories, if you draw more than 4 tiles, then all of those tokens will be placed back in the drawbag, with another player having the opportunity to shake the bag before a single replacement tile is drawn.

Carcassonne

 Learn to Play Carcassonne
  • You may only draw tiles when it is your turn. Do not pre-draw tiles.
  • We will be playing the base game only, scoring: knights (cities), travelers (roads), monks (monastaries), and farmers (fields)

Cascadia

 Learn to Play Cascadia
  • Wildlife tokens must be drawn from the bag one at a time.
  • If more than one is ever drawn at once, then all of those tokens will be placed back in the drawbag, with another player having the opportunity to shake the bag before a single replacement tile is drawn.
